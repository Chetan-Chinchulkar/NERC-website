Tentative Events North East Research Conclave 2022

Day 1

  • Inauguration of North-East Research Conclave
  • Patron(s) Meeting with Head of the Institutions/Vice Chancellor’s/Director’s for Sensitization of R&D Ecosystem in the North Eastern states.
  • Head of the Institutions Meet for Enhancement of North-East Research Infrastructure
  • Inauguration of North-East Research & Innovation Exhibition, Media Interaction
  • Plenary & Motivational R&D Talks by Acclaimed Researchers and Industrialists
  • Parallel Technical Sessions, Poster Display, Visit of School Children
  • Speakers: Research Scholars and Faculty members from all Universities and Technical Institutes of North-East India and East-India.
  • Cultural Program

Day 2

  • Brain Storming Sessions & Panel Discussions (Panellist: Directors/Vice Chancellors/ Senior    Government Officials/Officials from Funding agencies)
  • Theme 1: Hand-holding Mechanism for Sustained Research & Innovations Among North-East Institutions
  • Theme 2: Harnessing Traditional Knowledge of North Eastern States
  • Theme 3: Conservation of Biodiversity through R&D Interventions
  • Theme 4: North East Disaster Management & R&D Interventions
  • Theme 5: Government R&D Policies for North Eastern States
  • Theme 6: A Road Map for Standardization of Grass-root Innovations through R&D practices
  • Parallel Technical Sessions, Poster Display Contd.
  • North-East Research & Innovation Exhibition Contd.
  • Industry- Academia R&D Session
  • Dedicated Session on North-East Industry-Academia R&D Ecosystem & related Policies;
  • Speakers: Top Industry Officials & Policy Makers

Day 3

  • Session on Start Up & Perspective Entrepreneurs of North-East States
  • Open Floor Meeting of NE-Grass Root Innovators, Perspective Entrepreneurs with
  • Government Officials, Head of the Institutions
  • PRESS Meet to convey what are the achieved milestones of the Conclave and strengthening of the ties between academic Institutions & Industries
  • Valedictory Session of North-East Research Conclave
