Research Areas:

Following societally relevance research areas will be focused during North-East Research Conclave:


  • Agri and Rural, Traditional Technological Interventions
  • Food Processing, preservation and sustainable utilization of resources
  • Value-Added Products from Biomass/Bio-resource and Waste
  • Natural Disaster Management Technologies
  • Renewable and non-renewable energy resources
  • Advanced manufacturing, cutting edge Technologies
  • Health Care industries
  • Electronic manufacturing clusters
  • Tourism and eco-park development strategies
  • Indian Knowledge systems focused towards NER
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data science-based interventions
  • Efficient Management of Natural Resources
  • Other allied and focused sectors of NER Biodiversity
