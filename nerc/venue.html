Welcome to IIT Guwahati

The event will be held on the sprawling campus of Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG) which is situated on the banks of the majestic Brahmaputra River. IITG is an institute of national importance and is well known for its cutting edge research activities. It is also actively involved in the dissemination of technologies. Guwahati is the gateway of North East and is well connected by air, rail and road.

Guwahati Biotech Park

Guwahati Biotech Park(GBP) is one of its kind in the Northeast India and is a joint initiative of Science Technology and Climate Change Department, Government of Assam and Department of Biotechnology, Government of India, established in 2008. GBP has the state of art Technology Incubation Centre with multi-tenanted lab space, hi-tech equipment and support facilities, as well as developed land for large biotech players to set up their manufacturing units and create a self-evolving ecosystem of entrepreneurs and scientists.

Tourist destinations in the North-East

Assam (Asom, Axom) is a beautiful and diverse state with an incredible culture and beautiful landscapes located in the northeast of India. It shares its borders in the North and East with the Kingdom of Bhutan and Arunachal Pradesh. Guwahati lies between the banks of the Brahmaputra River and the foothills of the Shillong plateau, with LGB International Airport to the west and the town of Narengi to the east. It is gradually being expanded as North Guwahati to the northern bank of the Brahmaputra. The Guwahati region hosts diverse wildlife including rare animals such as Asian elephants, pythons, tigers, rhinoceros, gaurs, primate species, and endangered birds.
